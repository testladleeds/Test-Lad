How I work

Simple process, consistent outputs, no theatre.

1) Understand expectations

I take the product expectations from you (tickets, acceptance criteria, designs, stakeholder notes), then translate them into practical test coverage.

  • Inputs: User Stories, Acceptance Criteria, designs, analytics, prior bug history
  • Define risk: what breaks revenue, trust, conversion, or support load
  • Agree scope: what’s in / out for this round

2) Test + report clearly

You get actionable results: what failed, how to reproduce, and why it matters. I’ll work in your tooling (or provide a simple shared report).

  • Defects: steps, evidence, environment, expected vs actual
  • Coverage notes: what was tested and what was not
  • Quick comms: short check-ins, no status-meeting drag
